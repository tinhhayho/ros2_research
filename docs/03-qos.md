## The rule (Request-vs-Offered, "RxO")

A **subscription requests** the *minimum* quality it will accept; a **publisher offers** the
*maximum* it provides. They connect only if **offered ≥ requested** for every policy — the
publisher side must be the *stronger* of the pair. It is an **AND** across policies: fixing
reliability while durability still mismatches still delivers **nothing**.

Only **reliability, durability, deadline, liveliness** participate in compatibility.
**History, Depth, and Lifespan do NOT** affect whether two endpoints connect.
